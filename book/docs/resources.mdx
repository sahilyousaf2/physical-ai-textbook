- "ROS: an open-source Robot Operating System" - Morgan Quigley et al.
- "A survey of robot learning from demonstration" - Billard et al. (2008)
- "Simultaneous localization and mapping: A survey of current trends in SLAM" - Cadena et al. (2016)

### Bipedal Robotics

- "Capture point: A step toward humanoid push recovery" - Pratt et al. (2006)
- "Three-dimensional bipedal walking control based on divergent component of motion" - Englsberger et al. (2011)

## Getting Started Resources

### For Beginners
1. Start with the [Quick Start Guide](./quick-start) in this textbook
2. Follow the [ROS 2 Tutorials](https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/Tutorials.html)
3. Experiment with simulation environments before real hardware
4. Join ROS community forums for support

### For Advanced Users
1. Explore research papers in the areas of interest
2. Contribute to open-source robotics projects
3. Attend robotics conferences and workshops
4. Participate in robotics competitions
